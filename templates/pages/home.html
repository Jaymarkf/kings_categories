About Us

King’s Great Buys Plus is a leading retailer of electronics, appliances, beddings, and furniture since the opening of its first brick-and-mortar store in 1983. Three years later, the Great Buys Plus Corporation was founded in order to establish partnerships with other retailers and create a vast retail network. Today, with more than 40 stores spanning the Midwest, the King’s brand has become synonymous with high-quality products and excellent service. In early 2019, King’s launched its expanded online store to cater to customers from all over the country and beyond.
