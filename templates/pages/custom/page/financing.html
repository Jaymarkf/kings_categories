Apply For Financing

At Kings Great Buys Plus, we greatly honor the needs of our customers. To meet our customer needs, we offer a wide range of financing options. Whether it is regular financing through lenders such as Wells Fargo or other lending programs from other sources, we can help you get the item you need.

When applying for financing at one of our store locations, we normally give feedback within a few minutes. We can immediately provide you an answer as to your acceptance and the amount of your approved credit. If your application gets disproved, we can immediately work with our other lending sources to find a lender that will work with you.

A level of financial help like this is'nt available in the most stores, but with King's, it is our goal to offer the best shopping experience to our customers. To avail of this service, please drop by any of our locations today. Select the items that you want to purchase and allow us to help you find the financing option best suited for your needs.

If you have any questions regarding financing, it is recommended that you get in contact with your local store.
